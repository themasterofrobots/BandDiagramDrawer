About

Fixy is a mobile and web-based platform that allows property managers and city officials to better manage their operations and infrastructure improvements via crowdsourced information from citizens or residents.

Citizens or residents can submit instant, location-based reports of infrastructure issues in their community, which are posted for other users to view and upvote if they too are impacted. These reports will also be immediately directed to the relevant private or public agency.
